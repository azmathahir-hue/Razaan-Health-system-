About Razaan

A modern healthcare system built around every patient's chart

Razaan Healthcare System pairs experienced clinicians with a live digital charting platform — so vitals, progress notes and medications follow the patient, not the paperwork.

From registration to discharge, every department works from the same real-time record, cutting wait times and reducing errors.

  • ✓ Cloud-synced patient charts, accessible instantly
  • ✓ 15+ medical departments under one roof
  • ✓ 24/7 emergency and on-call physician coverage
